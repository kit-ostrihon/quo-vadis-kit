Why should you care?

{ Photo of Arthur's Seat by Lucija Ros }
  1. Full of heritage-listed buildings

How is it?

{ Photo of Calton Hill by Daniil Vnoutchkov }
  • A ruggedly handsome city with lots of character
  • Full of hidden gastronomic gems and award-winning restaurants, taking a modern take on the national dishes
  • Historical French ties influence the coffee scene in the form of patisseries
