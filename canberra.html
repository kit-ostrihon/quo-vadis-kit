How is it?

{ Photo of Canberra Balloon Spectacular by Harry Burk }
  • Sophisticated in its elegance, marrying brutalist architecture and nature
  • Intellectual haven with cafes for families and alternative folk
  • Established as a newly built city halfway between Melbourne and Sydney

What to do in Canberra?

{ Photo of National Arboretum Canberra by Harry Burk }
  • Explore the Lake Burley Griffin on a stand-up paddleboard
  • Have a picnic and a stroll in National Arboretum Canberra
  • Visit the world of miniatures via a visit to Cockington Green Gardens
  • Learn about the history of the land in the National Museum of Australia
  • Take a hike and inspect the city layout from the Mount Ainslie Lookout
  • Attend a glass-making workshop at Canberra Glassworks
  • Wander through National Gallery of Australia, and it's Sculpture Garden
